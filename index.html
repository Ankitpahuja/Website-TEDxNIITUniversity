To quote Oliver Wendell Holmes,

“Every now and then a man’s mind is stretched by a new idea or sensation, and never shrinks back to its former dimensions.”

Ideas are one of the most powerful things that the mankind has possessed. It has proven its might multiple times in many events of history. But these revolutionary ideas take a lot of time to get shared amongst the various communities across the globe for beneficiary purpose. Hence in 1984, Sir Richard Saul Warman took a stand and founded TED – an organization with the mission to spread ideas that will help shape a bright future for this global village that we live in.


TED is a non-profit organization that aims at spreading a worthy idea to millions of people in different corners of the world. From science to business to global issues – name it and TED has a list of related talks which have been delivered by experts. TED firmly believes in the power that an idea can have to influence opinions, attitudes and in the end, the whole world for a positive change. It strives to start a conversation on things that matter the most in our own lives and is a counsellor to the whole world.



TEDx is an international community that runs mini-TED-styled events in various institutes or towns. The ideas discussed in a TEDx event mainly cater to the pertinent issues that the community is facing or has faced in near past. The event may have live speakers presenting their ideas or may screen recorded TEDx talks delivered in the past which strike a resemblance to the current issues that the community/institution is facing or a combination of both.


The event is independently organized event by the community/institution under a license from TED and the ideas are free from political/religious/government issues. So do watch out for the next TEDx event near to you and help the community to reach to you with an idea that helps you alleviate from an issue.
